Playing Conditions

Tournament Rules

Format

Single league of 6 teams — every team plays each other once (15 matches). Top two on the points table meet in the final.

Squads

4 players per team. All players must be named at registration.

Innings length

League matches: 4 overs per innings. The final: 5 overs per innings.

Bowling limits

Maximum 2 overs one bowler only. Every team therefore needs at least 2 bowlers in a league match and at least 3 in the final.

Points & ties

Win = 2 points, tie or no result = 1 point. Positions on equal points decided by Net Run Rate. A tied final goes to a Super Over.

Spirit of cricket

Umpire's decision is final. Play hard, play fair — dissent or dangerous play leads to suspension at the organisers' discretion.
